1. IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR 2018)
Location / Date: Salt Lake City, June 18-21, 2018
Paper Submission Deadline: November 15, 2017
Acceptance rate: 20% ~ 30%
http://cvpr2018.thecvf.com/
２. IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV 2018)
Location / Date: Istanbul, Turkey; January 30-31, 2018
Paper Submission Deadline: November 30, 2017
Acceptance rate: 20% ~ 30%
http://www.guide2research.com/conference/iccv-2018
